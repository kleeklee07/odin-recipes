Chicken Adobo

Filipino Chicken Adobo

Here is one of the most popular filipino dishes. Usually consist of pork or chicken. Here we will be using chicken wings for this recipe.

What you'll need:

  • Chicken wings
  • Soy sauce
  • Peppercorn
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaf
  • Vinegar

Steps:

  1. Cut up your wings.
  2. Place the wings in a big pot.
  3. Pour in some soy sauce and vinegar (2/3 ratio soy sauce to vinegar) I usually do enough until half to two thirds of the chicken is submerged.
  4. Smash 4-5 cloves of garlic and place into pot.
  5. Put in some peppercorn.
  6. Put in 3-4 bay leaves
  7. Let marinate about an hour. Mix every 15 minutes.
  8. Place pot on high heat.
  9. Once it starts boiling bring down to medium/medium low. Stir often
  10. Cook about 45mins-1hour.
  11. Serve with white rice
