Pork Belly Tacos

Here are some really tasty and easy to make Pork Tacos. Just recently made this and I was a huge fan.

What you'll need:

  • Pork Belly
  • Soft taco wraps
  • Red Cabbage
  • Red Onion
  • Salt
  • BBQ seasoning
  • Paprika
  • White Vinegar
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Olive Oil
  • Lime
  • sugar

Steps:

  1. Cut up your onion and put in a container with half apple cidar vinegar and half white vinegar. Add salt and sugar. Let sit in fridge until ready. (longer the better)
  2. Cut up your Pork belly into small 1" cubes.
  3. Season with bbq season, salt, and Paprika.
  4. Grab skillet and heal to medium high.
  5. Oil up the pan w/ some olive oil.
  6. Once pan is hot, throw in the pork.
  7. Cook until fully cooked.
  8. Get the cabbage and cut into strips.
  9. Heat up your taco wrap.
  10. Put all together.
  11. Add a little dash of lime on top.
