Grilled Wings

One of my favorite summer foods. Chicken wings grilled on the grill. Here is an easy simple meal to make for summer or game days.

What you'll need:

  • Chicken wings
  • Seasoning of your choice (I love Dirty Bird)
  • charcol
  • lighter fluid
  • lighter

Steps:

  1. Cut your chicken wings into indiviudal wings.
  2. Season chicken with prefered seasoning.
  3. Prehead grill.
  4. Put chicken wings on.
  5. Be sure to watch chicken often, so you don't heavily char the chicken wings.
  6. Once chicken is over 165 degree internal temp, you may remove them.
